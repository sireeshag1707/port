The Central Hotel is a smaller four-star business hotel, which offers excellent location and full comfort. The hotel is only a step away from the main bus and railway stations. The sights of Ljubljana and the old city centre are accessible on foot. The hotel has 71 comfortable rooms and 3 suites. If you travel by car you can use the parking garage in close vicinity of the hotel.
